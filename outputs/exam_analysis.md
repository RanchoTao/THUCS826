## What can be known from filenames and accessible metadata

- The repository currently has year-based recall materials for 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020.
- These files are labeled as historical `912` materials, not `826` materials.
- The 2019 filename includes `综合`, suggesting a comprehensive recall file, but no question-level claims should be made without parsing the file.
- DOCX and PDF files may require separate extraction tools before detailed analysis.

## What cannot be safely concluded yet

- Exact question statements.
- Exact score distribution.
- Complete subject coverage in each year.
- Whether each recall file is complete or partial.
- Whether any file has been modified from its original source.

## Known gaps

- No current verified 826 past papers are present in `exams/`.
- No parsed question-level index exists for the 2017–2020 recall files.
- No official admissions/catalog source files are stored in `references/` or `docs/`.
- Older historical exam documents remain in the raw legacy archive and have not been normalized into `exams/`.
